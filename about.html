About Me

Hello, this is Mieko and I’m originally from Japan & a front office banker-turned-UX designer. As a banker I specialised in FX, loan/deposits and payments. I have 10 years of client experience, highly skilled in listening to client business needs, negotiating and building relationships. The key to good client communication is to investigate an issue that the client is facing and finding a solution that will satisfy the customer. I apply the same principles in my UX design and provide a creative solution to the problem. Now, I’m looking forward to applying the same client empathy skills to my UX design in a digital product.
On my days off, I spend my time with my daughter who just turned 2 years old. She’s recently learnt how to sing “ABCs” so don’t be frightened if you see me humming the song on my own when I’m around. Other than that, I like to get my sewing machine and alter my clothes or invite friends around and socialise over a glass of wine.
